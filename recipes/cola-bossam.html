Bossam





Description

This is the recipe of Bossam, it is a Korean traditional food.

I cooked this for my gf and her family, they liked it. (they're Europeans)

Caution for Muslim people : this meat is pork!



Ingredients

  • Pork (Picnic shoulder or blade shoulder) 700 g
  • Soy sauce 200 ml
  • Onion 1 ~ 2 EA
  • Cola 700 ml
  • Black peppercorn 10 ~ 15 pieces
  • Whole garlic 5 ~ 10 cloves


Steps

  1. Pour the cola 700 ml into the pot with pork 700 g
    2. the ratio between pork and cola -> cola 100 ml = pork 100 g

  2. Put the soy sauce into the pot.
    3. the ratio between cola and soy sauce -> cola 3 : soy sauce 1 you can add water 300 ~ 500 ml into the pot with soy sauce and cola.

  3. Put the black peppers and garlics into the pot.

  4. Simmer it for 10 minutes with high heat, then reduce the heat to medium and simmer for 30 minutes.

  5. Heat off and steam it for 20 minutes.

  6. Slice them to your desired size.

  7. Kimchi is really good combination with this food!
