Corn Cheese

Description

This is a recipe of corn cheese. (Korean style)

It's easy to cook, let's try it!

Ingredients

  • Canned corn [1 Can]
  • Mayonnaise [1 T]
  • Butter [1 T]
  • Sugar [0.5 T]
  • Mozzarella cheese [As you much as you wish]
  • Parsley [1 T]

Steps

  1. Drain the corn on the colander, toss it with 1T of melted butter.
  2. Put the mayonnaise into the frying pan and swirl it around.
  3. Toss the sugar with the corn and place it in a ovenproof dish.
  4. Top with lots of mozzarella cheese and sprinkle with parsley if you have it.
  5. After 10 minutes in the preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius, tadah~ Corn cheese!
